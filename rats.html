About Us

In New York City,the common unifier among the rich, the poor, and all other states of being, is the exposure to rats. Also death and taxes. But rats plague our communities, our buildings, and our city. They spread disease and eat our food. However, there are many ways to deal with rats in a way that ensures their wellbeing. Catching rats within your home with many of the non-violent traps available in todays society is now more feasible than ever. Once you catch a rat, you can release it into the wild without inflicting any undue suffering upon what is ultimatley an innocent creature.

Rat Calculator (NYC only)

After an untold amount of research a proper equation to determine rat count near you has been created

R=(P*e/D)*S*L/C

(Note there are some additional behind the scenes variables to provide the most accurate model)

  • R is estimated rat number
  • e is eulers constant
  • P is population density per square mile
  • D is distance in miles from nearest subway
  • S is total square mileage being asked about
  • L is love felt towards rats within area on scale of one(least love) to ten(most love)
  • C is cleanliness within area on scale of one(least clean) to ten(most clean)
