Rats, Cannibalism, and our Community

In New York City, the common unifier among the rich, the poor, and all other states of being, is the exposure to rats. Also death and taxes. But rats plague our communities, our buildings, and our city. They spread disease, eat our food, and have even killed several cats. One strategy to deal with them would be to do as pirates used to do, specifically by putting a bunch of rats together and having them get hungry and eat each other. These rats get a taste for rat blood and would then hunt down other rats.

Rat Calculator (NYC only)

After an untold amount of research a proper equation to determine rat count near you has been created

R=(P*e/D)*S*L/C

  • R is estimated rat number
  • P is population density per square mile
  • e is eulers constant
  • D is distance in miles from nearest subway
  • S is total square mileage being asked about
  • L is love felt towards rats within area on scale of one(least love) to ten(most love)
  • C is cleanliness within area on scale of one(least clean) to ten(most clean)
