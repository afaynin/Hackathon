Animals Without A Home

In the United States, homeless animals number in the millions. You may know of strays in your neighborhood - maybe one has crossed your path, or the pickings of a hungry animal has been spotted lying about. These animals deserve just as much respect as any pet. We as a community have to fight the cruelty shown in their abandonment. Talk to those at local animal shelters and see what you can do to help.

Adopting Wisely

Are you sure you can adopt and care for an animal? It is a very involved process, as you are letting another living creature into your life.

Every year, over six million animals are abandoned or lost. Don't contribute to this statistic, adopt responsibly!

Community and Awareness

When we inform other about the responsibiliity they have to accept when adopting a pet, we help ensure those pets aren't abandoned and left out in the streets. Together, we can make a difference. And should you ever feel you cannot care for your pet, do not abandon it. Look for a friend to take it, or see about returning your pet to a shelter. Many abandoned pets fail to survive as strays, and suffer immensily before they die. Don't put your pet through that. Adopt responsibly, return responsibly.
