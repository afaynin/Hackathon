About Us

Caring for Animals

Both of us have always loved animals. From winning a goldfish at a carnival, to currently having 2 playful fun cats next to my computer right now, we care deeply about the many animals hurt by all forms of neglect. We created this website in part to participate in the hackathon but also to inform our community about the numerous animals in need of help. Adopting from a shelter is one of the most rewarding experiences one can have, and we want to spread this joy to both pet owners and pets. So, what are you waiting for, visit your local shelter today!

Seth's Personal Goal

My goal is to work professionally in cybersecurity. But more than just that, I want to be able to contribute to those around me, both humans and animals. I volunteer regularly at a local animal shelter near me, and I am always eager to teach others about our little furry friends. I believe it is of vital importance to get this information out their to the community, so that before a person adopts a pet they are prepared to take care of it entirely, and not abandon it when it may become challenging. After all, this challenge leads pet ownership to be such a satisfying process that benefits both the pet and the owner. One day I would like to start my own business, and I am certain I can use part of my profits to help animals and the community around me flourish. In invoking this cause I can give the community something to join together in, and nothing would make me happier.
